The Detective's Dilemma

Welcome to The Detective's Dilemma, a technical game competition consisting of 3 stages:

  • Hostage
  • Time Bomb
  • Murder Mystery
    • In this game, teams will be tested on their technical skills, teamwork, and problem-solving abilities.

    A team can have a minimum of 2 members and a maximum of 4 members. Each team must have a unique name.

    Eligibility Criteria: Entry is open for all Engineering and Polytechnique students.
